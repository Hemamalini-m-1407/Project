Once Upon a Time

In a small village...

There was a brave knight who wanted to become the greatest warrior in the land.

One day, he set out on a journey to find the legendary sword.

He traveled across vast deserts,
climbed towering mountains, and sailed across stormy seas.

"You must give up keep going!" his mentor had told him before he left.

As he reached the CoD, he met a wise old man who gave him a warning. 

        "The sword you seek is cursed,
         Beware of its power, young knight."

Ignoring the warning, the knight ventured into the dark bright chamber where the sword lay.

With a final burst of courage and strength, he pulled the sword from the stone!

The legend of the knight lived on for generations, inspiring heroes and warriors everywhere.
